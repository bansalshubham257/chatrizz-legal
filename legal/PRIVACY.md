# Privacy Policy for ChatRizz

**Last Updated: June 27, 2026**

Welcome to ChatRizz. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
- **Profile Data**: When you add a match, you provide names, ages, bios, and interests.
- **Messages**: We process the chat messages you import or type to generate AI suggestions.

### b. Information Collected via Overlay Feature
- **Screen Content**: If you enable the optional floating overlay, tapping the overlay captures a screenshot of your current screen. This image is processed entirely on your device using on-device OCR (Google ML Kit) to extract text — **the raw screenshot image is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted**.
- **Extracted Text**: Only the text extracted from the screenshot may be sent to our AI backend to generate conversation suggestions.

### c. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We may collect basic device and OS information for crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics).
- **Usage Data**: We collect anonymized usage statistics via Firebase Analytics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- **AI Generation**: Your chat and profile data are used to generate personalized dating replies via AI.
- **Service Improvement**: Analytics help us understand how users interact with the app to improve features.
- **Monetization**: We show ads to free-tier users via Google AdMob.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage**: Most of your match and message data is stored locally on your device using Hive.
- **AI Processing**: Text is sent to AI providers (e.g., Groq/Google) for processing. We do not sell your personal conversation data to third parties.
- **Overlay Screenshots**: Screenshots captured via the overlay feature are processed entirely on-device using Google ML Kit OCR. The screenshot image exists only in temporary device memory and is immediately discarded after text extraction — it is never saved, uploaded, or shared.

## 4. Permissions
- **Overlay/Draw over other apps**: Required to display the floating overlay bubble above other apps. This allows you to trigger screenshot capture without leaving your messaging app.
- **Screen capture**: Required to capture the current screen content when you tap the overlay. The capture only happens on your explicit tap — there is no continuous or automatic monitoring of your screen.
- **Control**: You can enable or disable the overlay feature at any time from the app settings. Permissions are requested only when you first enable the overlay.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We use the following services:
- **Google AdMob**: For serving advertisements.
- **Firebase**: For analytics and crash reporting.
- **AI APIs**: For generating conversation suggestions.

## 6. Your Rights
You can delete your matches and clear your local data at any time within the app.
